## 1. Posting Cadence: The 100-Day System Design Challenge

**Cadence: 2 carousels/day + 1 Reel every 3 days**

This is the sustainable sweet spot — high enough volume to dominate the algorithm, low enough to maintain quality and siliconvalley.unni's personality.

### Daily Schedule (U.S. tech audience, PST)

| Slot | Time (PST) | Content Type | Tone |
|------|-----------|--------------|------|
| Morning | 7:00 - 8:30am | Educational carousel | "The Teacher" — clean, structured, memoji explaining |
| Evening | 7:00 - 9:00pm | Personal-angle carousel | "The Friend" — "Something I've been thinking about..." storytelling wrapper |
| Every 3rd day | Afternoon 12-1pm | Reel (30-60 sec) | Hot take, 60-sec summary, or "I tried X" clip |

### The Math
- 100 days x 2 carousels = 200 carousel slots
- 100 days / 3 = ~33 Reels
- Trending weeks (Week 1-3): 3 carousels/day to maximize launch momentum
- Evergreen weeks (Week 4+): 2 carousels/day for sustainability
- Total: ~233 carousel posts + 33 Reels = covers 300 topics + some recap/milestone content

### Personality Anchors (Preventing Audience Fatigue)
Morning post = structured education. Evening post = personal story wrapping the concept.
- First-person stories: "When I was working on our wearables AI pipeline..."
- Empathy hooks: "I know this sounds intimidating. Let me break it down."
- Encouragement: "If you understood this card, you're ahead of 90% of engineers."
- Real vulnerability: "I got this wrong for 2 years before someone explained it this way."

### "Comment to Get the PDF" Strategy
Every milestone post (every 25 topics) offers a free PDF:
- CTA: "Comment 'SYSTEM' to get the free PDF"
- Use ManyChat or DM automation to auto-send the link
- Drives comments (algorithm boost) + builds DM list

## 3. Algorithm Hacking with Volume

### Instagram's Signal Hierarchy (2025-2026)
1. **Saves** — highest-value signal for educational content
2. **Shares** (DMs and stories) — heavily weighted since 2024
3. **Reach-adjusted engagement** — engagement/accounts reached ratio
4. **Watch-through on Reels** — 70%+ threshold for broad distribution
5. **Comments** — especially long comments and back-and-forth

### Anti-Cannibalization Rules
- Never post two carousels on the same subtopic in one day
- Alternate "teach" and "challenge" formats within the same day
- Evening Reel references morning post: "If you haven't seen today's explainer — go back and save it"

## 9. Risk Management

| Risk | Cause | Mitigation |
|------|-------|------------|
| Shadow banning | Spammy behavior, low engagement | Rotate hashtag sets, monitor reach, drop to 1/day for 3-5 days if suppressed |
| Audience fatigue | Same tone too much | Three-act structure (teach/apply/debate), never two "lectures" same day |
| Quality dilution | Too much volume | Batch produce 4 weeks ahead, template system, combine thin topics |
| Losing personality | Content factory mode | Friday personal post — "Here's how [concept] came up in SV this week" |
| Burnout | **Most likely failure mode** | Build 4-week buffer before starting, assembly-line production days |
