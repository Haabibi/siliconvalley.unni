# System Design Mastery: 300-Topic Curriculum

**The Promise:** Follow this account for 100 days and you will be a system design expert.

**The Strategy:** We lead with what engineers are obsessing over RIGHT NOW -- vibe coding, MCP, AI agents -- then build foundational depth as you are already hooked. Senior engineers stay because it is never boring. Juniors grow because context accumulates.

**Structure:** ~14 weeks, ~25 topics per week (2 carousels/day + 1 Reel every 3 days), strategic difficulty curve.

**Posting Cadence:**
- Morning carousel (7-8:30am PST): "The Teacher" — structured, educational
- Evening carousel (7-9pm PST): "The Friend" — personal story wrapping the concept
- Every 3rd day Reel (12-1pm PST): Hot take, 60-sec summary, or "I tried X" clip
- Weeks 1-3: 3 carousels/day (launch sprint for algorithm capture)
- Weeks 4+: 2 carousels/day (sustainable rhythm)

**FOMO Mechanism:** Each topic includes a Day counter (Day X/100). If you are reading this mid-series, you can see exactly how many concepts you have already missed. The algorithm rewards accounts that make people feel urgency -- this does both.

Topics marked with [EXISTING] are from the current 114-topic collection.
Topics marked with [NEW] are additions.
Topics marked with [MILESTONE] are checkpoint/synthesis topics.
